Featured Anime

Vinland saga

Vinland Saga" follows Thorfinn, a young man seeking revenge for his father's death, as he journeys through a violent Viking world, evolving from a bloodthirsty warrior to a man striving for peace, inspired by his father's ideals.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a Japanese anime series about humanity's fight against giant, man-eating Titans. The story follows Eren Jaeger and his friends as they join the military to destroy the Titans.

Solo Levelling

"Solo Leveling" follows Sung Jin-woo, the weakest hunter, who gains the ability to level up and becomes a powerful hunter in a world where portals called "Gates" connect to monster-filled dungeons, and hunters with special abilities protect humanity.
